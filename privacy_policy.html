Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Kees ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services via WhatsApp.

Information We Collect

We only collect the phone numbers of delivery drivers and the grocery order details sent via WhatsApp. No other personal data is collected.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send order delivery details to drivers via WhatsApp
  • To ensure accurate and timely delivery of grocery items

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share any personal data with third parties. Information is only used internally for coordinating deliveries.

Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect the information transmitted through WhatsApp. However, we cannot guarantee complete security as no digital transmission is 100% secure.

Your Rights

If you are a driver and have questions about the use of your information, you may contact us at support@kees.app.
